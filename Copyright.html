Bugs Bunny first arrived in "Porky's Hare Hunt", showing early designs of his character. In this cartoon Bugs is displayed more frantic, and acts more like an animal. This changes later into 1940's Bugs Bunny, where we are met with what we see in the present day Bugs Bunny.
