This project uses material from "Porky's Hare Hunt" (1938) for educational and analytical purposes, specifically to discuss the early development of Bugs Bunny.

An Early prototype of Bugs Bunny first arrived in "Porky's Hare Hunt", showing early designs of his character. In this cartoon Bugs is displayed more frantic, and acts more like an animal compared to later portrayals. By the time Bugs Bunny is more established in the 40s, the character had developed the calmer, clever personality that audiences recognize today.
